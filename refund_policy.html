ZIL: Cancellation and Refund Policy

1. Since all orders are custom designed as in step with the patron’s requirement and are perishable in nature, refund on any cancellation isn't viable on the same day.

2. It is needed that 1-day prior note of cancellation be given for ordinary desserts and 2-days prior note accept for Large or Special Cakes. Refund expenses of 5% according to order shall be applicable.

3. Cancellation of Order will be mailed via way of means of the patron at care@ZIL.com with the Subject : “ Cancellation Request” and specifying withinside the mail :

i). Order ID

ii). Reasons for Cancellation

4. ZIL will issue a refund to the same card/account/medium through which you have made the payment incase of cancellation

5. All refunds will be made withinside the duration of 7-10 operating days from the date of cancellation.

6. It is completely at ZIL’s discretion, to change,cancel, refund or in any other case provide some other choice to the patron.
